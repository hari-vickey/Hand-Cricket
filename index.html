Rules

  1. Click on the Start button.
  2. Choose to Bat or Bowl.
  3. To play a number, Press the number shown in the image or Click on the hand image.
  4. After your play, the computer will play a number.
  5. You have three wickets to score and to defend.
  6. If the number played by the player and the computer is the same. Then, it is considered as a wicket.
  7. After three wickets, based on the toss you will bat or bowl.
  8. Finally, scores are compared to decide the winner.
